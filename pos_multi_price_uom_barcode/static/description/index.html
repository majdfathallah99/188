FlexiUnit POS: Dynamic Pricing & Barcodes

This module enhances Odoo POS to support multiple Units of Measure (UoM) per product with custom prices and dedicated barcodes. It enables businesses to sell products in different units (e.g., bottles, packs, or kilograms) directly from the POS, with automatic price adjustments and accurate inventory tracking.

Gaps Filled by the Module

  • Allow products to have multiple UoMs (e.g., "Each," "Box of 12").
  • Assign unique barcodes and prices to each UoM.
  • Streamline checkout by scanning UoM-specific barcodes.
  • Ensure accurate margin calculations and stock moves based on selected UoM.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Retail Store Selling Beverages

Product: Soda (Base UoM: "Bottle", Price: $2).
Multi-UoM Setup:
• "Pack of 6" (Barcode: 123456, Price: $10).
• "Case of 24" (Barcode: 789012, Price: $36).

Workflow:
• Cashier scans 123456 → POS adds 1 pack (6 bottles) at $10.
• Customer requests 2 loose bottles → Cashier selects "Bottle" from UoM button, adds 2 units at $2 each.

Scenario 2: Bulk Food Sales

Product: Rice (Base UoM: "Kg", Price: $5/kg).
Multi-UoM Setup:
• "500g Bag" (Barcode: RICE500G, Price: $2.50).
• "5kg Bag" (Barcode: RICE5KG, Price: $22).

Workflow:
• Scanning RICE5KG adds a 5kg bag to the order. Inventory deducts 5kg.



Multi-UoM Product Configuration
  • Define alternative UoMs (e.g., "Pack," "Kg") with custom prices and barcodes.
  • Restrict UoMs to the same category as the base product UoM.
Barcode-Driven UoM Selection
  • Scan UoM-specific barcodes to auto-select the unit and apply its price.
POS Interface Integration
  • UoM Selection Button: Manually choose UoMs during checkout.
  • Dynamic price updates based on selected UoM.
  • Prevent merging order lines with different UoMs.
Accurate Inventory & Reporting
  • Track stock moves using the selected UoM.
  • Margin calculations factor in UoM ratios (e.g., margin = subtotal - (cost / UoM ratio)).
Custom Label Printing
  • Print barcode labels for each UoM with its price.
Refund Compatibility
  • Refunds retain the original UoM and price for accuracy.
